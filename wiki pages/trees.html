Trees

Overview

Trees are the main way of obtaining wood in The Tribes. There are 4 differant types of trees. Oak, Cypress, Rimu and Dacrycarpus. trees can be harvested by punching them repeatadly until they have been fully destroyed. every time you hit a tree, there is a 25% that a piece of wood corosponding to the type of tree will appear nearby. when a tree has been fully destroyed, there is a 10% that a fiend will spawn.
