Added Vector Icons(25/10/2024)

Overview

Today I used inkscape to create vector icons for a couple of differant things.

I want this game to not include tons of icons and text that a few rpgs and other games have because that kinda breaks the immersion. This isnt nessesarily a bad thing for all games and games like runescape or world of warcraft benfit from lots of information because of how complicated the game is. however for my game, I wanted to creat an immersive survival experiance, to achieve this I am going to try to have very few text and icons and have optional icons and text toggleable in settings. There are going to be a few inevitable things that Im going to need to make icons for such as health so that is what I focused on today.
