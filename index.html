Game Overview

You spawn into a dense forest with no memory of how you got there. You are severely injured but alive. It is up to you to survive by foraging resources and crafting tools to thrive in your new environment. As you adventure you notice strange monolithic structures with unfamiliar language. You uncover strange dungeons and creatures that are completely unfamiliar to you unlike the surface which gives you a slight sense of Déjà vu. As you continue building your base, you meet people like you who you can recruit to join your mission to uncover the truth.

Target Audience

This game is for people who want to experience a true survival experience without the unrealistic building mechanics. This game involves time-consuming harvesting and building similar to how real-life survival would work. This game is also for people who like VR games.
